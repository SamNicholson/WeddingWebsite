Our Story

One day in October 2009 the happy couple met at the Fox and Newt pub in Leeds. The bride, Emily, had arranged to meet up with her friend Sean Gordon who she had not seen since they were visiting Egypt in 2004. Along to the pub Sean had also invited a handful of his friends that included his house mate, the groom, Samuel.

After a few months of getting to know each other Emily and Samuel officially became a couple on the 9th January 2010. After three years of student life Emily and Samuel’s relationship still stayed strong despite the obvious north south divide. Having now settled into graduate life in Leeds Samuel can now be classed as an honorary northerner.

5 years later on the 20th December 2014 Emily and Samuel were walking down to Friars Crag, Keswick with Killiebrae Mickey. Unknown to Emily, the night before Samuel had hidden a series of geocache boxes to aide his elaborate proposal. Once the couple reached the final box containing the ring Samuel got down on one knee and asked her to marry him. In complete shock Emily said ‘Yes’ whilst Killiebrae Mickey was oblivious of the moment.
